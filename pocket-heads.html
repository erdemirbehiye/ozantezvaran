Pockets/Heads

2022

“POCKET/HEADS” provides an insight into the strict dichotomy between the safe spaces of our personal pockets and the outside world as the camera takes on the role of an observer.

Photography/Video/Music: Ozan Tezvaran.
Graphics: @popi_pissouriou
